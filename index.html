How I look at a website

Not everything wrong with a site is a design problem. Not everything is even wrong.

RED

If it stops someone from converting, that's not a style opinion — it's a business problem, and it gets fixed first.

BLUE

Some things aren't broken. They're just not doing their best yet. Worth improving — never urgent.

GREEN

Good work deserves to be left alone, not redesigned for its own sake. I tell you what's already working too.
